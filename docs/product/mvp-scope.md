# MVP Scope

This MVP builds an AI-assisted recruiting workflow for initial screening and structured interviews.

AI workflows are blocked until the organization connects and verifies its own model API. Platform-default model fallback is disabled.

The MVP includes:

- Job rubric approval before screening.
- Resume evidence display.
- Screening recommendations with evidence or missing-information flags.
- Interview guide generation tied to approved criteria.
- Interview summary validation and human confirmation.
- Compliance alerts and audit export.

The MVP excludes automated rejection, protected-trait inference, social-media scraping, facial analysis, emotion analysis, and compensation recommendations.
